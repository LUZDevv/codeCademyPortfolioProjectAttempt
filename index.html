BIO

"A little about yours truly!"

ABOUT

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Luis Uribe Zambrano and I am an upcoming self-taught web/UX developer.

Recently graduating from the University of Washington Bothell (BA - Interactive Media Design - MCL honors), and partaking in some programming courses, I ended up discovering a passion for putting things together and having multiple ways to do so - by using code, in my case.

I have delved a bit in the area since my teens: by attempting to modify games and sites, for, totally-serious reasons, usually.

TOOLS

Bringing a unique culmination of self-taught education and some computer science material during my college stint - I offer a main focus on Web development (front-end, thus now), UI/UX design, research, and consultation.

Some tools that I currently use often are: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, git/github, CLI, Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and node.js.

I am currently learning: React.js/native, back-end development (data structures + algorithms), and further lessons on all the above and beyond.

MISC.

If you would like to contact me, please fill out the form on my ‘CONTACT’ page or see me on LinkedIn. During downtime (not including 'studytime'), I like to hang with close people, play racing video games, work on music, or simply relax.

My goal is to help create practical (with a tinge of 'fun' involved) and helpful products into reality, while constantly adapting to the new possibilities each year. I also look forward to the fun collaborations and efficient teamwork that will take place in building these realities.
